• At the beginning of Stage 1, you and your co-participant will be given an endowment of 100 tokens.
  • You and your co-participant can each keep your tokens for yourself, or use them to buy virtual “lottery tickets”.
  • You and your co-participant can each buy 1 lottery ticket with 1 token.
  • The number of lottery tickets you and your co-participant buy determines your chance of winning a prize (details on next screen).

Decision: You and your co-participant must each decide how many tokens to use to buy lottery tickets. Participants will retain tokens not used to buy lottery tickets.
