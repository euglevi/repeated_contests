If you win the prize: you will earn the tokens you did not use to buy lottery tickets, and the prize won from buying lottery tickets.

Your earnings from Stage 2 = Your endowment – Number of tokens used to purchase lottery tickets + Prize

If you do not win the prize: you will only earn the tokens you did not use to buy lottery tickets.

Your earnings from Stage 2 = Your endowment – Number of tokens used to purchase lottery tickets



Your final earnings = Your earnings from Stage 1 + Your earnings from Stage 2

Your final earnings will be converted to cash at a rate of {{ Constants.exchange_rate }} tokens = $1.
