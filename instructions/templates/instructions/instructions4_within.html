If you win the prize: you will earn the tokens you did not use to buy lottery tickets, and the prize won from buying lottery tickets.

Your earnings = Your endowment – Number of tokens used to purchase lottery tickets + Prize

If you do not win the prize: you will only earn the tokens you did not use to buy lottery tickets.

Your earnings = Your endowment – Number of tokens used to purchase lottery tickets

Example

You and your co-participant each receive an endowment of 25 tokens. Suppose you use 4 tokens to purchase 4 lottery tickets and your co-participant uses 12 tokens to purchase 12 lottery tickets.

There is a 4/(4 + 12) = 25% chance one of your tickets will be chosen and you win the prize.

Suppose you win the prize. Stage 1 earnings would be:

You Your co-participant
Endowment 25 tokens 25 tokens
Tokens used to purchase lottery tickets - 4 tokens - 12 tokens
Prize + 100 tokens + 0 tokens
Total Stage 1 earnings = 121 tokens = 13 tokens

Suppose you use 4 tokens to purchase 4 lottery tickets and your co-participant uses 12 tokens to purchase 12 lottery tickets.

There is a 12/(4 + 12) = 75% chance one of your co-participant's tickets will be chosen and you do not win the prize.

Suppose your co-participant wins the prize. Stage 1 earnings would be:

You Your co-participant
Endowment 25 tokens 25 tokens
Tokens used to purchase lottery tickets - 4 tokens - 12 tokens
Prize + 0 tokens + 100 tokens
Total Stage 1 earnings = 21 tokens = 113 tokens
